3. STRATEGY RECALIBRATION — CRITICAL

3.1 The 5% SL Problem
- 5% SL on $50k account at 1x leverage = $2,500 loss = 5% of equity = INSTANT MDL BREACH
- At 5x leverage: 25% account loss per stopped trade = catastrophic
- Solution: Decouple technical SL from equity risk
- MUST reduce position sizing: 1% equity risk per trade → $500 risk on $50k
- With 5% technical SL → max position = $10,000 = 0.2x effective leverage
- Only 20% of funded capital actually deployed = fee drag still significant but manageable

3.2 Consistency Rules Problem
- 8 trades/month = lumpy PnL distribution
- Single 4% day can = 40% of total evaluation profit → violates 30% consistency rule
- Solution: Only use firms WITHOUT consistency rules (Breakout Prop, HyroTrader post-eval)
- OR: Hardcode profit cap in bot logic

3.3 Minimum Trading Days
- 8 signals/month may not meet 10-day minimums
- Solution: Use firms without minimum trading days (Breakout Prop)

4. TAX & REGULATION (GERMANY)

4.1 Income Classification
- Prop payouts ≠ capital gains. Trader is independent contractor providing signal service to foreign entity
- §15 EStG (Gewerbliche Einkünfte) = most likely classification for automated 24/7 bot
- §22 Nr.3 EStG (Sonstige Einkünfte) = unlikely to hold for systematic bot
- This is ACTUALLY BETTER than §20 EStG because:
  - No €20,000 derivative loss offset cap
  - All business expenses fully deductible (VPS, evaluation fees, API costs)
  - Trade tax Freibetrag = €24,500/year

4.2 Trade Tax (Gewerbesteuer)
- Must register Gewerbeanmeldung
- Freibetrag: €24,500/year for Einzelunternehmen/GbR
- Above that: 3.5% × Hebesatz (300-500% depending on city)
- Münster Hebesatz: ~450% → effective rate ~15.75% above Freibetrag
- Portion credited against personal income tax

4.3 VAT (Umsatzsteuer)
- B2B service to foreign entity → Reverse-Charge Mechanism
- Invoice without German VAT, with Steuerschuldnerschaft note
- Must acquire USt-IdNr.
- Evaluation fees: fully deductible as Betriebsausgaben (even failed evaluations)

4.4 Loss Offsets
- If business expenses exceed payouts in a year → loss offset against other active income (e.g. salary)
- Evaluation fees = fully deductible Betriebsausgaben, even if failed
- Total abolition of €20k derivative loss offset cap (§20 Abs. 6 EStG) = massive structural advantage

4.5 PROFIT SPLIT REPORTING
- 80/20 split: Trader reports ONLY the 80% received as gross revenue, NOT 100% of simulated profits
- The 20% retained by prop firm is never reported by the trader

8. RED FLAGS & DUE DILIGENCE
============================================================

1. **Tick-by-Tick Trailing Drawdowns** = PREDATORY
   - Open equity float counts toward drawdown
   - Trade floating +4% → closes +1% = 3% drawdown counted
   - MUST use EOD or static balance drawdown firms (Breakout Prop!)

2. **B-Book Synthetic Feeds** = AVOID
   - Artificial spread widening during volatility to trigger SLs
   - Must verify "Real Exchange Execution" / A-Book routing

3. **Vague Prohibited Practices** = RED FLAG
   - "Latency arbitrage" or "exploitative strategies" = intentionally vague
   - Can be weaponized to deny payouts retroactively
   - CFT's <30s trade ban is clear and acceptable (we hold 24h)

4. **Payout Denial Waves** = CRITICAL RISK
   - FundingPips: recent backlash for terminating accounts over disputed "tick scalping"
   - Suppressed negative reviews on Trustpilot
